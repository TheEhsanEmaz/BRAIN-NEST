FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to create an account?
No — just enter your existing student ID and password. Your account is set up by the department automatically when you enroll in the university.
Can I register from my phone?
Yes. The portal is fully responsive and works on any modern browser on phones, tablets, and computers.
What happens if I register for a course by mistake?
You can drop any course from your dashboard at any time during the registration window. Just click Drop next to the course and confirm.
How many courses can I register for?
The maximum is 18 credit hours per semester. Your dashboard shows how many credits you have left to use so you never go over the limit.
Is my registration saved if I close the browser?
Yes. Registrations are saved locally in your browser so your enrolled courses persist between sessions on the same device.
